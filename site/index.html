What makes CrossPet special

Everything you need for great reading — plus the things that make it uniquely CrossPet.

Core Reading

  • EPUB 2 & 3 with image support
  • Anti-aliased grayscale text rendering
  • 3 font families × 4 sizes × 4 styles
  • Multi-language hyphenation engine
  • KOReader Sync — cross-device progress
  • WiFi book upload + Calibre + OPDS browser
  • 4 screen orientations (Portrait, Landscape CW/CCW, Inverted)
  • Fully remappable physical buttons
  • Custom sleep screens (cover art, custom images)
  • SD-first aggressive layout caching

CrossPet Exclusives

  • Virtual Chicken — pixel companion that grows with your reading
  • Reading Stats — daily time, streaks & session tracking
  • Stats sleep screen dashboard
  • Weather Widget — Open-Meteo API, shown in header & clock
  • Clock + Lunar Calendar with month navigation
  • Daily Literary Quotes on clock sleep screen
  • BLE Remote & Presenter — keyboard & slide control
  • Mini Games — Chess, Caro, Sudoku, Minesweeper, 2048
  • Pomodoro Timer
  • Vietnamese fonts — full diacritics, anti-aliased on e-ink
🇻🇳

Vietnamese fonts that actually work on e-ink

CrossPet includes 3 font families (Bookerly, Noto Sans, Bokerlam) with full Vietnamese diacritics support — ă, â, đ, ê, ô, ơ, ư and all tone marks render perfectly with anti-aliased grayscale on the 800×480 e-ink display. Bokerlam is a handcrafted Vietnamese font optimized for readability. No more broken characters or missing glyphs.

More than just a reader

Useful tools built right into the firmware — no apps, no internet required.

🕐

Clock & Lunar Calendar

Monthly calendar with lunar date integration. Navigate months with Left/Right buttons. Vietnamese lunar calendar shows "Âm lịch" range in header. Perfect for tracking traditional holidays.

🌤

Weather Widget

Current weather from Open-Meteo API — free, no API key needed. Shows temperature in header bar, clock sleep screen, and dedicated weather app. Auto-syncs when WiFi connects.

📝

Daily Literary Quotes

28 rotating literary quotes displayed on the clock sleep screen. A new quote every day to inspire your reading journey.

🍅

Pomodoro Timer

Built-in focus timer for productive reading sessions. Set work/break intervals and stay on track — right on your e-reader.

📡

Your e-reader is also a wireless presenter

Connect to your laptop via BLE and control presentations with physical buttons — next slide, previous slide, from across the room. No dongle, no app. Just pair and present.
